Statutes for the Parish Pastoral Council

Diocese of Limerick (2016)
The following are drawn from the Statutes for Parish Pastoral Councils in the Diocese of Limerick.

Eligibility to Nominate
& to Vote

  • Any parishioner who has completed his or her 18th birthday and has received the sacrament of Confirmation shall be eligible to nominate candidates for the PPC and to vote.
  • Parishioners should be registered members of the parish.

Eligibility to Hold Office

  • Any parishioner who has completed his or her 18th birthday and has received the sacrament of Confirmation and enjoys a good reputation shall be eligible for nomination and election.
  • Those who accept nomination should be willing to serve.

Nomination Process
(For a Valid Nomination)

  • There is no limitation to the number of parishioners who may be nominated as candidates for election to the PPC.
Our Common Mission
The Parish Pastoral Council exists to assist the parish in fulfilling its mission of evangelization, worship, community and service, in communion with the Church in the Diocese of Limerick.
